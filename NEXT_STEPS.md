## 7. Use The Cottonwood Harbor Standard Before Release

Before publishing any page, product, feature, or resource, answer yes to all of these:

- Does it help businesses run better?
- Is it clear and easy to understand?
- Does it reflect our brand?
- Would we be proud to show it to our largest future customer?
- Does it earn trust?

If any answer is no, keep improving.
