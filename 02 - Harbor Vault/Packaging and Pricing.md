## Pricing Philosophy

Harbor Vault is designed for Organizations that want enterprise-quality software without enterprise complexity.

Whether you are a solo consultant or a growing company with nearly 100 employees, you use the same integrated platform with the same powerful capabilities.

Our goal is simple:

Help Organizations spend less time managing software and more time doing meaningful work.

## Product Rules

Do not launch with Professional and Business tiers.

Do not create a complicated feature matrix.

Do not make seat count the headline. User limits are implementation details; plan positioning should speak to Organization stage.

Do not build enterprise-only features before the core 1 to 100 user experience is excellent.
