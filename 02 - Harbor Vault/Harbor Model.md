# The Harbor Model

## Purpose

The Harbor Model is the organizing concept for Harbor Vault.

It gives the product a simple mental model that can support many industries without becoming generic or chaotic.

## Core Idea

Every business, regardless of industry, can be represented by five core concepts:

1. Organizations
2. People
3. Work
4. Knowledge
5. Insights

Everything in Harbor Vault should fit into one of these categories.

## The Center Of The System

Harbor Vault revolves around Organizations.

Everything belongs to an Organization.

Organizations can contain:

- People
- Work
- Documents
- Assets
- Locations
- Notes
- AI summaries
- Activity
- Invoices later
- History

This makes Harbor Vault stronger for B2B customers than a contact first CRM.

## 1. Organizations

Organizations are the businesses, customers, vendors, partners, or entities a company works with.

Organizations are the primary record type.

Primary question:

What is the current state of this relationship?

## 2. People

People are contacts, employees, stakeholders, decision makers, and internal team members.

People usually belong to an Organization, but internal team members may belong to the user's own company.

Primary question:

Who is involved and what do they need?

## 3. Work

Work is anything that needs to be planned, tracked, completed, renewed, resolved, or followed up.

Do not force users into "tasks," "jobs," or "projects."

Work can become:

- Service call
- Project
- Estimate
- Installation
- Inspection
- Renewal
- Onboarding
- Follow up
- Issue

Primary question:

What needs to happen next?

## 4. Knowledge

Knowledge includes documents, notes, history, templates, standard operating procedures, files, conversations, and context.

Knowledge turns scattered information into a usable operating memory.

Primary question:

Where is the information I need?

## 5. Insights

Insights include reports, AI briefs, business health signals, risks, recommendations, trends, and suggested next actions.

Insights are where Harbor AI becomes a business advisor rather than a chatbot.

Primary question:

What is Harbor Vault seeing that I might miss?

## Relationship Model

```text
Organization
  People
  Work
  Knowledge
  Insights
  Activity
```

## Product Rule

If a new feature does not fit the Harbor Model, rethink the feature.

If a screen shows information but does not clarify Organizations, People, Work, Knowledge, or Insights, rethink the screen.
